// Production Process

From Raw Materials
to Finished Product

01
Raw Material Procurement

Sourcing Natural Ingredients

Rice flour, corn starch, seaweed extract, fruit pulp, and plant proteins are sourced and quality-checked. These materials are ground into fine powder or paste using industrial pulverizers and homogenizers, then blended with natural plasticizers like glycerol for flexibility.

02
Edible Film Formation

Creating the Wrapper Film

The blended solution is cast into ultra-thin sheets using a precision casting machine, then passed through a drying tunnel (hot air or infrared) to form uniform edible films. These are rolled into continuous edible film rolls — just like plastic rolls, but entirely safe to eat.

03
Strengthening & Coating

Moisture Protection & Flavoring

A thin layer of edible wax (beeswax or carnauba wax) is applied via coating machine for moisture resistance. Optional natural flavors (chocolate, mint, fruit) are sprayed on. UV sterilization ensures food safety standards are maintained throughout.

04
QR Code Printing

Printing with Edible Ink

Food-grade edible ink (same technology used in cake decorating) is used to print unique QR codes and brand logos directly on the wrapper using an edible inkjet printer. Once dried, the QR code is fully readable and completely safe to consume.

05
Wrapping & Sealing

Packaging the Snacks

Snacks (brownies, bars, cookies, chips) are fed into a modified flow wrap machine that handles edible film rolls. Edges are sealed with food-grade adhesives (gelatin or starch paste) using gentle heat/pressure. A cutting machine trims and shapes each pack.

06
Quality Control

Testing & Certification

Each batch undergoes rigorous testing: tensile strength testing for durability, moisture analysis for barrier effectiveness, and shelf-life simulation. Products are tested for FSSAI and ISO food safety compliance before release.

07
Distribution

Packaged & Ready to Ship

EcoSnap-wrapped snacks are packed into 100% biodegradable cartons using a cartoning machine. Boxes are weighed, sealed, and palletized for delivery to canteens, food outlets, and FMCG partners — the complete zero-waste supply chain.

// Purpose · Vision · Mission

Why EcoSnap Exists

Purpose

EcoSnap exists to eliminate single-use plastic packaging by transforming food packaging into something that is not wasted — but consumed or naturally returned to the earth. We make eco-friendly choices effortless, innovative, and economically viable for brands and consumers alike.

Vision

To create a world where packaging leaves zero waste behind and sustainability is embedded into everyday consumption. EcoSnap envisions becoming a global standard for edible and biodegradable packaging, helping industries worldwide shift from plastic dependency to planet-positive solutions.

Mission

To design and produce safe, edible, and biodegradable packaging using natural food-grade materials. To partner with food brands and FMCG companies to replace plastic. To educate consumers through interactive QR codes and reduce plastic pollution while ensuring affordability and food safety.
